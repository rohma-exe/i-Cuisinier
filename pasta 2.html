Pasta

Exquisite linguine, expertly al dente, enveloped in a velvety tomato reduction infused with aromatic herbs, delivering a refined Italian culinary experience.
Watch Video

Ingredients

  • 1. Flour
  • 2. Eggs
  • 3. Olive Oil
  • 4. Salt

Directions

  1. On a clean, flat surface, mound the flour.
  2. Crack the eggs into the well, add the olive oil and salt.
  3. Using a fork, gently beat the eggs, oil, and salt together, gradually incorporating the flour from the edges of the well.
  4. Once the mixture becomes too thick to mix with the fork, use your hands to knead the dough.
